Let's Get Started: Gameplay

For each challenge, there will be a riddle pointing to a significant piece of Miss MmmButterBears and ChickenScratch's history, hobbies, or favorite things to do.

  • Solve the riddle to get to the next checkpoint.
  • Click SOLVED and cast your spell.
  • Retrieve the missing artifact.
